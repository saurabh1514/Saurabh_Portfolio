To be honest, I started my web development journey after I completed my graduation.In today's time this sounds little weird.Everytime I come across people who are junior to me they say , I want to become a web developer. perhaps developer these days are having a great time. Demand for good developers is sky rocketing and hence their pay as well is sky rocketing. So came to my journey or should I say , How I develop interest in web development coming from Electronics and Telecommunication background. I did not web development until final year of my enginnering ( I have just known about HTML, CSS etc is used to build websites or apps , no implementation).After completion of my final year I have decided to give a chance to learn web development. I did a course on fundamentals of web development and in that course period I find that I am enjoying this learning phase and I have develop interest as well. After completion of course,I have applied for internship for the same role and I got the internship.During the internship, I learn and develop small components and it was that moment that I really started enjoying. Sometimes we get overwhelmed about learning so much about web development but give yourself a time , understand the basics , fundamentals and Practice Practice Practice. Over the time you will develop confidance and you will grow everyday and also learn everyday.
